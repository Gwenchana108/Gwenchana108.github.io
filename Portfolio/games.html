The Inventor's Puzzle

This game was created in Twine. It's an interactive game that teaches math and logic skills. As the player, you and your parents have been summoned to the house of your grandfather, who is missing. Can you solve the clues and discover where your grandfather has gone?


Click on the picture above if you are up to the task!




To create this game, I wrote the story, developed simple logic puzzles, and created the art in Procreate and Adobe Photoshop.

The image above is the game map from Twine! I color coded it to keep the different threads straight as I was working on it.



The Botanical Force

This game was created in Construct 3. Botanical Force is a game that allows learners to explore the game environment, while evaluating and collecting resources to cultivate plant growth and selectively propagate plants with desirable characteristics to produce up to 3 new hybrid breeds.

Click on the picture above if you would like to be part of the Botanical Force!


This game was part of a team effort. I co-designed the storyline and level development. I designed the characters and created the artwork in Procreate, and then programmed the game in Construct 3.





Rare Finds


This game is also a group effort. It was created to teach Social Emotional Learning(SEL) concepts in a fun, almost stealthy way! the player finds that they are running a pet shelter that is inhabited by Rare Finds. The player must observe their adorable Rare Find, and with the occasional help from friendly NPC's, identify their little pet and then find the perfect home for it!.


For this game I developed the storyline and artwork.
